The Challenge

Climbing Mount Everest is one of the most difficult challenges in the world. Climbers must deal with low oxygen levels, freezing temperatures, and extreme exhaustion.

Required Equipment

  • Climbing gear
    • Oxygen tanks
    • Thermal suits
    • Ice axes

The “Death Zone” above 8,000 meters is where the human body cannot survive long. Every step becomes harder, and decision-making becomes critical.
