Welcome to Mount Everest

Mount Everest is the highest mountain on Earth at 8,848.86 meters above sea level. It is part of the Himalayas and lies between Nepal and Tibet. It was formed millions of years ago due to the collision of tectonic plates.

This website explores its history, geography, climate, climbing challenges, and amazing facts about the mountain.
